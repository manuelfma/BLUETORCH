# Blue Torch.me Project Memory

## Product
Blue Torch.me is an enterprise B2B fintech platform for venture capital, growth equity, and private equity investment teams. It supports end-to-end deal review from intake to investment memo export.

## Audience
Investment professionals: analysts, associates, principals, partners, operating partners, platform teams, and investment committee members. Users are financially literate, time-constrained, skeptical, and responsible for high-stakes decisions.

## Voice
Professional, concise, analytical, and trustworthy. Use clear claims, structured reasoning, precise labels, and caveats when evidence is incomplete.

## Core Workflow
Deal intake, private document upload, parsing, source-linked evidence extraction, risk assessment, diligence question generation, human review, memo drafting, and export.

## AI Rules
AI outputs must distinguish facts, assumptions, estimates, risks, recommendations, open questions, and missing evidence. Each output must include citation, confidence, evidence type, missing information, and review state.

## Anti-Goals
No hype, no fabricated facts, no final investment decisions, no legal/tax/accounting advice, no promises of superior performance, and no uncited memo-ready claims.
